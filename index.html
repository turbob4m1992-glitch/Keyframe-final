Frequently asked

What does a Keyframe project cost?+
Projects typically start around $5,000 USD for a focused custom site. Cinematic builds run $8,000 to $25,000 and platforms start at $15,000. Every project is quoted fixed-scope within two business days of a discovery call; we never bill hourly.
How long does a website take?+
A custom cinematic build typically takes six to ten weeks, depending on the number of staged scenes, 3D complexity, and content readiness. Focused custom sites move faster. Your fixed-scope quote comes with a real timeline.
Do you design in Arabic?+
Yes. Arabic is designed alongside English from day one, never translated after. That covers bilingual identities, RTL web and mobile experiences, and Arabic-first campaigns across Jordan and the GCC.
Where is Keyframe based?+
Founder-led from Amman and Doha, serving Jordan, the GCC, and the USA. The founder works directly on every project; there are no hand-offs to juniors.
